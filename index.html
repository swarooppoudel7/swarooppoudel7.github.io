ABOUT ME

I am a recent graduate of Beloit College (class of '22), majoring in Psychology and Business Economics with a minor in Computer Science. I am starting the position of Behavioral Research Fellow at Northwestern University this summer. I'm interested in understanding decision-making in the context of health and wellbeing at the individual level looking at persuasion and habit formation to establish healthier lives. Taking this a step further, I am also interested in understanding fit and belonging at an organizational level and decision making at the organizational level to improve employees' health and wellbeing in the workspace. I am also currently working to bring academic research and literature with easy-to-digest snippets in the form of a podcast: Understanding your decisions with Swaroop Poudel.
